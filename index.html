Salary Report

The 2015 AAPA Salary Report is the most detailed snapshot of PA compensation and benefits information available.

  • Clear, easy-to-read tables on topics such as base salary, base hourly wages and bonuses for a variety of variables such as state, specialty, employer and setting.
  • In-depth information about benefits commonly offered to PAs, including paid days off, professional development, insurance and retirement benefits, as well as additional forms of compensation.
  • Perfect for students, employers and researchers seeking to negotiate new employment contracts, offer fair compensation packages and enhance their data sets on the profession.
